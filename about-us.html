Who we are

Onshore Construction Company Pvt. Ltd (OCCPL) was formed in the year 1995 by Mr. Belle Seetharam Shetty, Chairman & Managing Director, with a vision to build a large scale client-centric company in the construction industry.

OCCPL has executed over 250 projects in the core construction sectors all over the county and abroad. Being an ISO certified company and strength in resource allocation, OCCPL is not only surging ahead in core sectors but getting involved in revolutionary projects. In just over two decades, OCCPL has turned out to be a trusted name with clients and consultants.

To rise on every occasion with confidence, capability and competence to achieve global acceptance as one of the leading EPC companies with client’s faith and confidence.

MISSION

  • To perform for our customers at the highest level of quality construction services with fair & market competitive prices.
  • To ensure the longevity of our company by achieving customer satisfaction in all areas, including attention to details, service-minded attitude, and timely completion.
  • To maintain the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty, and fairness in our relationships with customers, suppliers, subcontractors, and professional associates.
  • Giving utmost importance to health, safety and welfare of our employees.

STRENGTHS

  • Excellence in handling EPC contracts as well as offering construction services with proficiency in large scale piping, heavy equipment erection, structural, tankages, heaters and reformer work.
  • Large fleet of cranes and other self-owned machineries
  • Trusted workforce with excellent skills and confidence.
  • Well qualified & experienced supervision teams.
  • Expertise in handling big plant revamp and shutdown work
  • Well established manufacturing facilities in India and abroad
  • Strong management team with competence, confidence, and commitment to move towards the set growth plans.
